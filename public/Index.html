Biography

Formed in 2009 by Brothers Nick and Lucas Harasz with Jared Caprio originally under the name Megalith. Frostfang is a St. Petersburg Florida heavy metal band that attempts to follow in the footsteps of their influences while hopefully forging a new path. Frostfang started out as a fast thrashy ode to the 80s metal giants. But with the addition of Evan Ford, quickly evolved into experimenting with longer flowing and jamming epics more inspired by psychedelic rock, stoner rock/metal, and post rock/metal. We don't really know what "subgenre" of metal we fit under, and like the freedom to create what feels good without a verbal mandate of what style we should pursue.
